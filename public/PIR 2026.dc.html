Enrollment Context · Action Matrix

Decreasing & Fluctuating Enrollment

1 · Programs Implemented+
  • Early Registration Campaign
  • Balik-Eskwela & Oplan Balik-Eskwela programs
  • Learner Information System (LIS) updating and validation
  • School-based enrollment advocacy and orientation activities
  • Coordination with parents and barangay stakeholders during enrollment
2 · Limitations+
  • Limited reach in remote/coastal areas → delayed or missed enrollment information and low awareness
  • Limited counseling personnel; family financial difficulties; learner mobility (relocation/employment)
  • Low parent participation (work schedules, language barriers, weak engagement structures)
  • Limited budget for sustained campaigns and outreach
  • Transportation and geographic barriers in far-flung areas
3 · To Strengthen+
  • Early Registration & Continuous Enrollment Advocacy Program
  • Oplan Balik-Eskwela institutionalization and expansion
  • Strengthened Community and Parent Engagement Program
  • Learner Tracking, Retention, and Re-enrollment Monitoring System
  • School-Community Partnership Strengthening (SGC + Barangay linkage)
4 · To Implement / Introduce+
  • LOMP — Learner Outreach & Mapping Program: locate remote learners, track enrolment annually
  • CBEAT — Community-Based Enrollment Advocacy Teams (teachers, barangay partners, parent leaders)
  • Home-Based Learning Support & Counseling for struggling learners and at-risk dropouts
  • JHS→SHS Continuity Pathway Program to reduce transition-year dropout
  • Integrated Literacy + Retention Intervention Program (reading gains ↔ retention)
5 · Why Not Fully Addressed by the SDO+
  • SDO works within approved budgets from higher government, which may not fully cover campaigns, transportation, personnel, and outreach
  • Enrollment is driven by socio-economic factors (poverty, migration, relocation) needing LGU, social-welfare, and barangay collaboration beyond the SDO mandate
  • Limited division manpower and competing priorities constrain field monitoring, child-finding, and sustained technical assistance
6 · Programs to Sustain+
  • JESP — Enrollment Sustainability & Partnership Program: stronger LGU/barangay/parent partnerships, a yearly sustainability plan, and stakeholder mobilization for retention
  • Learner Assistance & Retention Support: identify learners needing support, refer/link to agencies, and strengthen school-based interventions for vulnerable learners
